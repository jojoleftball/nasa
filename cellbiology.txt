

## 1. Transcription profiling of Jurkat T cells to study the effect of clinorotation on the expression of apoptosis-related genes
**Description**: This study examined how simulated microgravity (via clinorotation) affects gene expression in human Jurkat T cells, focusing on apoptosis pathways. Cells showed altered expression of genes like BCL2 and CASP3, revealing how space conditions disrupt programmed cell death and immune cell function, crucial for understanding astronaut immune suppression.
**Year**: 2004
**Authors/Scientists**: Thomas J. Nickerson, C. Mark Ott, Satish K. Mehta, Cheryl A. Kerwin
**Institution**: NASA Johnson Space Center
**Mission**: N/A (Ground-based clinorotation simulation)
**OSD number**: OSD-13
**Tags**: apoptosis, T cells, simulated microgravity, gene expression, immune cells
**OSD link**: https://osdr.nasa.gov/bio/repo/data/studies/OSD-13
