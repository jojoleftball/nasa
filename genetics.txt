
## 1. Gene-expression profiling of Saccharomyces cerevisiae irradiated by high-LET radiations
**Description**: This study profiled gene expression changes in yeast (Saccharomyces cerevisiae) exposed to high-linear energy transfer (high-LET) iron radiation versus low-LET gamma radiation, identifying alterations in DNA repair, cell cycle, and stress response genes to model cosmic radiation effects on eukaryotic cells for space travel.
**Year**: 2007
**Authors/Scientists**: Louis S. Stodola, Cheryl A. Kerwin, John W. Criddle
**Institution**: NASA Ames Research Center
**Mission**: (Ground-based radiation simulation)
**OSD number**: OSD-97
**Tags**: Saccharomyces cerevisiae, transcription profiling, high-LET radiation, DNA repair, cosmic radiation
**OSD link**: https://osdr.nasa.gov/bio/repo/data/studies/OSD-97
